WCPDB

World Confederation of Parents of Beadblind is a non-governmental, charitable and non-profit making worldwide organization. It is composed of several Federations, Associations and Bodies that give attention to deafblind people and their relatives. It aims for a worldwide representation of the whole “association” from the point of view of the relatives.

EDbN

European Deafblind Network is a nonprofit entity whose scope of work is DEAFBLINDNESS, this is a disability resulting from the combined loss of vision and hearing sense. The welfare and equal opportunities for people with this disability and their families are the reason of the existance of EDbN. At last, EDbN represents the interests of people with deafblindness who join us.
